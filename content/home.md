Research Software for Infectious Disease Epidemiology

We are a research software engineering team working within the [MRC Centre for Global Infectious Disease Analysis](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/mrc-global-infectious-disease-analysis) and the [Department of Infectious Disease Epidemiology](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/school-public-health/infectious-disease-epidemiology) at Imperial College London.

We work with researchers to improve public health outcomes by co-developing innovative software, including high-performance modelling tools, immersive visualisation portals, and data submission and storage solutions. Our goal is to make both cutting-edge research and routine tasks easier, more reliable, and more accessible for public health practitioners at Imperial and its partner organisations worldwide. In short, we aim to maximize the amount of science done per line of code.
